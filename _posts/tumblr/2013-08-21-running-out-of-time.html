What a weird band Iceage is. These Danish dudes look like they just got out of high school; they’ve been associated with neo-nazism ever since I heard from them; their two albums have been unanimously praised by critics worldwide; but most importantly, they’ve been producing some of most menacing and uncomfortable punk music of the new millennium.

I’m glad to see they can put on a good show, because they totally let me down when I saw them at Il Motore in Montreal back in July of 2012. The singer was a wreck and the rest of the guys just couldn’t get a song right. But then again, they’re pretty chaotic : that could’ve been exactly what they wanted. I just might give them a second chance, cause they’re that good.
