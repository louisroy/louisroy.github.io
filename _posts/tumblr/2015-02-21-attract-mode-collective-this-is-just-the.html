“THIS IS JUST THE BEGINNING OF WHAT WE CAN DO ON F*CKING SUPER NINTENDO”

elix is a two person combo comprised of cTrix, a chiptuner who hails from the land down under, and ferris, a Norwegian who creates EDM that spans multiple genres, including chiptunes.

Together they’ve been working on a project throughout 2014 that will hopefully be in the hands of fellow 16-bit musicians sometime this year, in the form of a public beta. And of what exactly? Why, a tracker for the Super Nintendo.

The video below is the first publicly posted demonstration, running on stock hardware. The audio you hear is raw audio output from the SPC700, the Sony produced sound chip found in every SNES. And that’s it; no fancy tricks:
