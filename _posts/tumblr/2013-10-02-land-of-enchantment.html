A week and half of fun in New Mexico, in no particular order :

  • visited a couple of Breaking Bad shooting locations;
  • watched my first rodeo show;
  • rode my first mechanical bull (and sucked badly at it);
  • met some seriously brilliant people;
  • drank some of the best IPAs I had in a while;
  • got to visit Roswell and it’s funny/mockable UFO museum;
  • hiked a little bit;
  • bathed in a hot spring;
  • saw a lynx

In short, this was somewhat unexpected to me, but New Mexico really is aptly named “Land of Enchantment”. Can’t wait to go back. I’ll post pictures soon.
